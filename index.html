About Our Ministry

Hope of a New Life Ministry is a Catholic charity organization based in Uganda, dedicated to transforming the lives of orphaned children through compassionate care and spiritual guidance. Rooted in the teachings of the Catholic Church, our mission is to provide a nurturing environment where vulnerable children can thrive physically, emotionally, and spiritually.

Our Vision

Hope of a New Life Ministry envisions a world where every orphaned child in Uganda has the opportunity to live with dignity, receive a holistic education, and grow in faith. By combining practical support with spiritual care, we aim to nurture the next generation of faithful, empowered individuals who can contribute to their communities.

Our People

Naminya Ahamada

Director

My name is Naminya Ahamada, aged 30 years old, Ugandan by nationality, driver by profession and have always been positive in every aspect of life.
I was raised from an extended peasant family where values of honesty, respect and hard work were emphasized.
Education was my priority though my family circumstances didn’t make it easy to excel. Beyond academics, I enjoy sports, watching football, making friends, carpentry, and volunteering as these help develop teamwork, kindness, honesty, leadership, and helpfulness.
As a dedicated and passionate individual, I co-founded a charity community-based organization and have had the privilege of volunteering with HOPE OF A NEW LIFE MINISTRY.
My contributions include: spiritual and biblical education, promoting hygiene and sanitation, supporting orphans and vulnerable children, enhancing human rights, and sensitizing people on environmental management.
